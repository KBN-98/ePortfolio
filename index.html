A little about me

  • Born in NSW, raised in central QLD.
  • My entire family is heavily involved with IT and gaming, we used to raid together in the MMO, World of Warcraft.
  • I graduated in 2015 and since then have been working in sales and purchasing.
  • I work in the construction and building supplies industry, with most of my time spent in mining and commercial sales.

My passion for IT runs deep, you could say it is in my blood. Both my parents and siblings are avid gamers and tech enthusiasts. This undeniably moulded me from a young age into the person I am today; I thrive on learning and adapting to new technology, and have a passion for analysing processes, finding a better way to do something whenever I can. While I am enthralled by IT, I am also all too familiar with the safety concerns that it entails, I’d like to work towards a safer world of technology for everyone.

Before applying for RMIT , I researched a lot of Australian universities, my main concerns being exciting course content and online support. Out of all the schools I had investigated, RMIT not only had performed well and ranked highly for STEM-related subjects but offered many options for online study. Also, there were many great reviews for RMIT from alumni, and also current students.

During my studies , I am excited to be challenged and to expand my knowledge as much as I can; I am expecting to gain a greater appreciation for IT and all its components. Information Technology encompasses a plethora of fields, some of which I am sure to have never heard of. Alongside exploring things I am already interested in; I’m enthused by the idea of fields unknown to me!
